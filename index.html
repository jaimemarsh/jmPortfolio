About Me

Hi! I'm Jaime Marsh :)

I excel at finding ways to break down big ideas or projects into smaller pieces and understand how everything works together. I bring logic, structure, and critical thinking to an idea alongside creativity, emotion, and design to make any big idea a reality.

After starting in Athletic Training/Coaching, and later discovering Web Development, my path shifted again into Content Creation, Photography, Social Media, Marketing, and Website Building. I felt like I was constantly being turned around trying to find where I could create, solve problems, and help people. When I combined all of these skills together I found myself at my most successful.

I love turning big ideas into experiences people can see, feel, and interact with, and I’m excited to keep going.
