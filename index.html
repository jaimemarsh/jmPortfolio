About me

Hi! My name is Jaime Marsh. I'm here to help you and your business succeed by creating a website from the ground up, fixing one that isn't what you want, or working for you on a longer project. I'm currently working in the Columbus, Ohio area, but have no problem working remote. I'm open to many opportunities and positions, including freelance work.

For your website I don't have a stash of layouts to use, but want to talk with you about your goals, needs, and features you want. Your business isn't like everyone else's, and your website shouldn't be either. Having a well organized website is hard, let me help.

I focus on front end development including the design and interaction of a website, and backend development including databases, security, and other server-side functions. Evaluate what you need. Feel free to contact me if you have questions.
